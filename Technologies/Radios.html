Retevis RT-777 / Baofeng BF-888s

Retevis RT-777 / Baofeng BF-888s
Start: $15 (Radio with basic accessories such as antenna, belt clip, charger, ect)
Custom Programming: +$5 (programming setup fee, additional radios with identical custom programming do not have this fee).

The RT777 (rebranded BF-888s) is the cheapest two way radio you can get capable of repeater use, aftermarket parts, and custom programming. The reliability is surprisingly decent for this mass produced "made in China" radio and is great in calm/safe situations, meaning it is NOT reccomended for emergency services, and can be used in survival situations but should carry an extra, do not rely on just one.

Retevis RT-777 / Baofeng BF-888s

Custom Programming: +$15 (programming setup fee up to 50 custom* channels, additional radios with identical custom programming* do not have this fee).

The RT5 (rebranded UV-5R) is a cheap, relitively high channel and functionality, two way radio. This is classified as a ham radio as you can input frequencies on-the-go (without PC programming).



Features/Specs:
Frequency Range (Tx): (VHF)136-174,(UHF-B/C/D)400-520MHz
Frequency Range (Rx): (FM Radio)65-108,(VHF)136-174,(UHF-B/C/D)400-520MHz (adjustable outside this band but quality may suffer on out-of-band frequencies)
Frequency Banding: 12.5/25kHz
Frequency Step: 2.5/5/10/12.5/25/50kHz
Tx Power: 2w/4w/8w transmitter (based on programming - can be set based on channel, and/or toggled with a programmed button)
Channels: 127 (digital selection button)
Repeater Capable: Yes

Kenwood 2-pin accessory connector (used with most Retevis/Baofeng radios)
Small LED "flashlight" on top. Not quite as bright as most cell phone flashes, but definitely useful in very dark environments.



Custom* channels are channels that are not common and in our default programming.
Example: Walkie Talkie channels, NOAA Weather, and MURS are all "common" and not "custom" channels to us.

Identical Custom Programming* is typically exactly as it sounds, however we will change the power on text between radios for free.
For example, the only difference between two of your radios may be one says "Leeks Rad" and one says "Spare 5" when you turn it on, we will count that as identical when billing.
